Understanding The Spread of COVID-19 in Ireland using the S-I-R Model and Logistic Growth Function

  • Category: Statistical Modelling, Machine Learning, Mathematical Modelling, Epidemiological Modelling, Data Analysis
  • Notebook Link: Google Collab Notebook
  • Tools Used: Python (Pandas, Numpy, Plotly, Scikit-Learn, Matplotlib, solve_ivp
  • Project URL: https://github.com/emil-sincliar/Understanding-the-Spread-of-COVID-19-in-Ireland

Project Aims and Objectives

-> To analyze and understand the spread of the COVID-19 outbreak using the epidemiological S-I-R model and the Logistic Growth Model

-> To understand how the virus is propagating in Ireland and if the measures of lockdown implemented have an effect of the spread of the virus

-> To simulate and forecast the propagation and transmission dynamics of the virus and to identify the general trends in the transmission behavior of the virus

-> To compare the results obtained from both these models and analyze and better understand these models in terms of epidemiology and data analytics
